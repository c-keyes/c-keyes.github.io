Directed Reading Program

I started a directed reading program (DRP) at Emory University and organized it from Spring 2021 until Spring 2023. The program pairs motivated undergraduates with graduate students to learn a topic together. See this page for more information.

Outreach

  • I co-organized Emory's Julia Robinson Math Festival on Saturday, August 27, 2022. We had over 150 people attend, including middle and high school students and their families!
  • I was an instructor with Emory Math Circle for 8 semesters from Fall 2018 to Spring 2023. I planned and led lessons for middle and high school students at beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels, serving over 200 students in the Atlanta community.
  • I was involved with with Emory Math Circle's Week of Mathematical Exploration in Summer 2019, a weeklong summer program which offers middle and high school students the opportunity to engage in mathematical research.
  • From 2015 - 2017 I worked with Tufts STOMP, a program that brings college students into elementary school classrooms to teach lessons on engineering and science.
